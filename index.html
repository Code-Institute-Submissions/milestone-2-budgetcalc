Welcome to BudgetCalc

This is a Web App created by Alexander Forrest to help you get control over your budget and see patterns in your spending.

You can use the navigation on the left to jump to a specific page or once you've filled the information on the current page you can use the next and previous buttons at the bottom of the page to jump from page to page.

Once you've filled in all the relevant data there is an option at the end to generate a report which will be sent to you by email.

Net Worth Overview

In this section you will get an overview of you currently available funds and outstanding debts. By filling in this information you can have a clear overview of your current net worth .

If you wish to skip this section and go straight to calculating your monthly budget click next below or select income from the Table of Contents on the left.
