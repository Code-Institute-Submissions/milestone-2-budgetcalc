Welcome to BudgetCalc

How does it work?

This is a Web App created by Alexander Forrest to help you get control over your budget and see patterns in your spending.

On each page you will be asked to fill in information about your current financial information. The information will be used to calculate your totals for each section aswell as provide you with a global overview of your finances on the final page. The more information that you fill in, the more complete that this overview will be.

If you wish to skip a section that your Budget Overview will only take into account the parameters that have been filled in.

Pie charts will also be automatically generated on each page so that you can have a better understanding about where you spend your money and you can use this information to make structural choices about how you spend you money.

At the bottom of the Budget Overview you will also get the opportunity to generate a report which will be you can then send to yourself (or someone else) by email.

Visualising Your Data

When you fill in the inputs on each page your data will automatically be graphed at the bottom of each page with Plotly. Plotly also gives you an option to save an image of the graphs that are constructed.

Navigation

There are two methods for navigation throughout the calculator. At the bottom of each page you will see a next and previous button which you can use to move forwards and backwards throughout the calulator. Alternatively you can use the Table of Contents navigation sidebar to go straight to any page, at any time.

How To Save Your Information For Future Use

Once you've filled on all the relevant data it is also possible to save your inputs and retrieve the data at a later time. This information is all stored locally on your computer and not sent to a server or anyone else.

You can do this by making use of the Save and Load buttons on the bottom right of the sidebar navigation.

Questions, Comments, Bugs and New Features

If you would like to get in contact with BudgetCalc because you have a question, comment, have found a bug or would like to request a new feature, please you the contact form here, alternatively you can acces the contact page at anytime from the navbar menu item in the top right: Report a Bug / Request a Feature.

Repsonsiveness

This web app has been constructed to work on any internet capable device; desktop, laptop, tablet or mobile phone. Please note that if you save your infromation this information is only saved locally, and as such, it can only be loaded from the same device that you saved it on.

Net Worth Overview

In this section you will get an overview of your currently available funds and currently outstanding debts. By filling in this information you can have a clear overview of your current net worth .

Please note that having a large amount of outstanding debt (due to a mortgage, car loan, etc.) filling this in does not influence your monthly budget calculations. This section is meant to create aweareness so that you all the tools you need to make clear financial planning decisions for the future.

At the bottom of the page a pie chart will be generated so you can visualise your current accounts and debts.
